Mexicorn Salsa

This recipe belongs to my friend Christina and her mom

Ingredients

For the Salsa:

  • 2-4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 can black bean, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can mexicorn, drained
  • 2 plum tomatoes, chopped small
  • 2 avocados, chopped
  • 1 lime (juice for avocado)
  • Tortilla chips or fried scoops

For the Dressing:

  • 1 packet good season Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup vinegar/li>
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 plum tomatoes, chopped small
  • 2 avocados, chopped
  • 1 lime (juice for avocado)
  • Tortilla chips or fried scoops

Steps

  1. Mix everything else overnight before.
  2. Add tomatoes and cut avocados and lime juice before serving.
